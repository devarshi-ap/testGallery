Central Park

New York City, New York.

  • Central Park was designed by Frederick Law Olmsted, America's greatest landscape architect, and Calvert Vaux in 1858; it was one of the first American parks to be developed using landscape architecture techniques.


  • Arguably one of the most famous parks in the world, Central Park occupies an area of 840 acres and extends between 59th and 110th streets (about 4 km). Not only is it the first public park built in America, but it is also one of the most frequently visited, with over 25 million guests per year.
