Red Mineral Temple

Digital Art, KVANT-1 Studio.

  • The experience of viewing KVANT-1's otherworldly photo series, 'The Temple' can be likened to entering a futuristic alien-like world, where the unexpected is celebrated.


  • A series of shimmering, blood-red crystals fall from the ceiling, creating a cave-like structure. Two figures, clocked in black sit like ants, shrunken by the vastness of the objects suspended above them
